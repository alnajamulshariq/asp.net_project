Courses & Labs Fees

Course Fees and Policies at Symphony Institute

1. Basic Course:
  • Fee: $4275
  • Duration: 4 Months
  • Includes foundational training and certification preparation.
2. Advance Course:
  • Fee: $6000
  • Duration: 6 Months
  • For students with prior knowledge, directly covering advanced topics.
3. Labs Practice Sessions (Optional):
  • Fee: $1000 (if opted during registration).
  • Fee: $1500 (if opted after course completion).
  • Provides additional lab hours and faculty guidance post-course.
4. Entrance Examination Fee:
  • Fee: $100

Fee Policies

1. Payment Deadlines
  • Course fees must be paid at least 1 day before the class commencement.
  • Entrance exam fees must be paid during the application process.
2. Method of Payment:
  • Payments can be made via challan.
  • Online payment methods are expected to be introduced soon.
3. Refunds and Cancellations:
  • No refunds will be issued once the course or lab sessions have commenced.
  • In case of cancellation before the course starts, the refund will be processed after deducting administrative charges.
4. Lab Access Charges:
  • Lab facilities during the course duration are free.
  • Post-course lab usage is chargeable as per the above-mentioned fees.
5. Fee Revisions:
  • All fees are subject to periodic revision and will be updated on the official portal by the admin.

By adhering to these policies, Symphony Limited ensures transparency and smooth financial management for its students.
